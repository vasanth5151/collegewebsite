About Placement


P.T.Lee CNCET provides its students the best in life, with placement centre functioning in the campus under the able administration with the assistance of an experienced faculty in each discipline. The very active placement centre serves as a foundation for many careers by guiding students in right direction through continuous counseling and arranging for campus recruitments.This centre creates a meticulous database and assists the students in getting placed in national & multinational companies through campus recruitment.

  • Importance of Research

Mission

  • To maintain a regular relationship with companies and train the students according to their expectations
